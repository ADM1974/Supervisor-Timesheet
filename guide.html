A quick overview for supervisors: how the hours get in, how you sign them off, and how payroll is paid.

How workers submit their hours

Contractors — green app. No Microsoft account. They open the app and enter their personal access code. Their site is already set. They add work orders + hours for each day, tick any extras, and Submit.

Your part — approving

Open the red app, sign in with Microsoft, and you'll see one card per person's week for your sites — their hours plus any extras.

  • Check it, then Approve to sign off.
  • Reject sends it back with your reason — the worker sees it, fixes their week (it re-opens for them, even if it had closed) and re-submits to you.
  • You can't approve your own — a senior approver does that, and you sign off theirs.
  • "All caught up" = nothing waiting.

📄 Payroll

The day after each site's close day, accounts automatically receives a spreadsheet + PDF of that site's approved hours (you're cc'd). You can also send it yourself from the red app — 📧 Email last week's payroll report — handy after late approvals. Only approved hours are ever paid.

Good to know

  • Each site has its own pay week (its own close day).
  • Work orders and extras/allowances are set up per site by the office.
  • The apps install on a phone like normal apps — open the link, Share → Add to Home Screen.

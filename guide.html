  • Check the hours and extras, then tap Approve to sign it off.
  • Tap Reject to send it back — you'll be asked for a quick reason.

  • Approved sheets disappear from the list. "All caught up" means nothing's waiting.

3 · Send to payroll (when the week's done)

Tap 📧 Email last week's payroll report at the top of the list — it emails your site's approved hours (spreadsheet + PDF) to accounts. This also happens automatically each week, so it's just a backup / for re-sending after late approvals.
